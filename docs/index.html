About Us

The Setar Cultural Association, registered in Zurich, is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting and preserving the rich cultural heritage of classical Iranian music. Our mission is to share the beauty and elegance of traditional Persian music with the community, fostering cross-cultural understanding and appreciation.

We achieve this through organizing high-quality concerts, featuring renowned musicians and emerging talents, as well as offering music courses and workshops for enthusiasts of all levels. Our goal is to create a vibrant and inclusive platform for music lovers to come together, learn, and enjoy the unique sounds and rhythms of Iranian classical music.

By supporting the Setar Cultural Association, you contribute to the preservation and dissemination of this precious cultural treasure, enriching the cultural landscape of Zurich and beyond.
