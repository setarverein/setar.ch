Classical Persian Music Night

Date: Sunday, February 23, 2025
Time: Doors open at 19:00 | Concert starts at 20:00
Location: Klus Park, Zürich
Tickets: Regular: CHF 35.— | Reduced (Students & AHV): CHF 25.—

Event Description

The Median Ensemble invites you to an unforgettable evening of classical Persian music. Experience the rich musical heritage of Iran through the masterful performance of traditional instruments and melodies.

The concert features authentic instruments including setar, tar, santur, and tombak, bringing to life ancient musical traditions in a contemporary context.

This special performance celebrates the classical Persian musical tradition known as the Radif, a collection of melodic figures preserved through centuries of oral tradition.
